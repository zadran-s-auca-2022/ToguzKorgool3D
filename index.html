Rules – Short Version

Each player has 9 pits with 9 korgools in each, and a kazan. If a pit has 1 stone, it moves to the next pit. If it has more than 1 stone, one stays and the rest are sown counter-clockwise.

If the last stone lands in an opponent’s pit and that pit now has an even number, they are captured to your kazan. When a player reaches 82 or more stones, the game ends. Basic tuz/sool rule is also supported.
